Ancient Mesopotamia

Mesopotamia, known as the cradle of civilization, is where writing, laws, and cities first emerged. It thrived between the Tigris and Euphrates rivers.

  • Timeline: 3500 BCE - 539 BCE
  • Key Features: Cuneiform, Ziggurats, Hammurabi's Code
  • Major Cities: Ur, Babylon, Nineveh
